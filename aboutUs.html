Our Journey

1995 - 2000

WheelHub was founded in 1995 with a vision to become a pioneer in automotive retail in the United States. At its inception, Wheel Hub focused on selling high-quality cars and accessories through showrooms spread across major cities in the United States. Within the first five years, Wheel Hub successfully built a strong reputation as a provider of quality cars with superior customer service.

2000 - 2010

Entering the 2000s, WheelHub began to expand its reach by opening more showrooms in strategic locations. In addition, the company also started adopting new technologies to improve operational efficiency and customer experience. In 2005, Wheel Hub launched a customer loyalty program that provides various benefits to loyal customers.

2010 - 2020

The next decade became a period of digital transformation for WheelHub. In 2015, Wheel Hub launched an official website that allows customers to view car catalogs online, check specifications, and even place orders directly. This move was part of the company's strategy to redefine the automotive shopping experience by leveraging digital technology.

2020 - now

In 2020, WheelHub launched a more advanced and responsive online platform. The website is designed to provide comprehensive information on the cars for sale, showcase the latest promotions, and provide customer registration services. With five main pages consisting of Home Page, Promotions Page, Listing Page, All Cars Page, and About Us Page, the site offers easy and intuitive navigation for users.
